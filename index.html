Research practice

How I approach UX research

I work across discovery, evaluative, and mixed-methods research — from understanding user needs and workflows to testing concepts, evaluating usability, and shaping product decisions.

01

Frame the decision

I start by clarifying what the team needs to learn or decide, then choose the right method and level of rigor for the question.

02

Build evidence

I combine qualitative and quantitative research to understand both what users say and how they behave.

03

Translate to action

I turn findings into product implications: what to improve, what to test next, where users need support, and which tradeoffs matter.
